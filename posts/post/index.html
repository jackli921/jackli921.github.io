First Blog - First Day of New Habits - Half Month into My 1st Dev Job#

Today is a special day for me.

It marks the start of our 5AM Project; The start of our attempt to build better habits and rid of bad habits.

We woke up at 5:30 AM. Did our morning routines. Studied what we always find tired of studying at the end of a work day.

I haven’t felt this good about myself in a long while. In the sense that my mind wasn’t incessently worrying or ruminating.

I felt relaxed and that’s partly thanks to the comfortable coach given tous by our lovely landlord, Mario.

I got started with this blog and this post today, too.

I also went out for my first HIIT run, which I hope to continue with my workouts at home.

Today is also Canada Day.

Go all out with each moment in the day that is now, but once the day passes, let it pass behind you.

Today is the start of a great transformation of ourselves, and I’m to be witnessing it.
